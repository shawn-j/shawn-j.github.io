SECTION 3 — NICHE-SPECIFIC VISUAL STYLES
===============================================================

VINTAGE EPHEMERA:
- warm sepia / parchment tones
- no bright colors; everything is muted
- torn edges, deckled edges, perforations
- typewriter fonts, ledger marks, postal stamps
- 20–40 elements per printable sheet
- mega mosaic thumbnails for huge bundles
- stacked sheets for medium bundles
- botanical greens/muted florals
- grunge textures, foxing spots, coffee stains
- buyer expectation: PRINTABLE SHEETS + PNGs (mixed)

PNG CLIPART:
- bright, clean icons
- white or pastel backgrounds
- 6–20 pieces per thumbnail
- mosaic collages for large sets
- hero-shot characters for character bundles
- crisp outlines, no grain, no distressing
- pastel palettes or full color saturation

SEAMLESS PATTERNS:
- grid of pattern tiles (8–20 tiles)
- pastel/boho neutrals OR vintage browns
- mosaic walls for mega bundles
- label strip with “DIGITAL PAPER” or file details
- no whitespace; patterns must dominate

WATERCOLOR CLIPART:
- soft blended strokes
- pastel palettes
- 6–12 items per thumbnail OR 20–30 for florals
- watercolor paper texture
- gentle gradients, botanical leaves, animals
- text overlays delicate (thin serif/sans)

DIGITAL STICKERS:
- Goodnotes style: pastel palette, rounded edges, clean vector look
- Functional stickers: icons in rows/grids
- Kawaii: bright, cute, thick outlines
- Meme/sarcastic: bold black text, simple design
- PNG-only bundles are okay here
